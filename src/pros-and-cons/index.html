Pros and Cons of a Psilocybin Mushroom Trip

Pros

Immediate effects:

  • Otherworldly experience

Effects lasting months or years:

  • Brings back simple joys of everyday life
  • Helps make peace with past traumas, fears, and worries
  • Non-addictive
  • Anti-addictive
  • Anti-depressant
  • Stimulates creative thinking

Cons

  • Psychological risk for unprepared individuals
  • Illegal in most places
  • Social stigma against illegal drugs
  • Can't be done quickly like beers after work
